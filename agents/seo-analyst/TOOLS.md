
## SEO Research Process

1. Identify target topic/niche
2. Search and analyze top 10 results
3. Extract common keywords and themes
4. Identify content gaps
5. Assess keyword difficulty and opportunity
6. Document findings and recommendations

## Notes

- Always check search intent (informational, navigational, transactional, commercial)
- Look for "People Also Ask" and related searches
- Consider content format (blog, guide, video, tool)
- Track SERP features (featured snippets, knowledge panels, etc.)
