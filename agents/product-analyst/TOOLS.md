# Shuri - Tool Notes

## Research Workspace

`/data/workspace/mission-control/agents/product-analyst/research/`

Create this directory as needed. Organize research by date and topic.

## Mission Control CLI

```bash
cd /data/workspace/mission-control
./scripts/mc.sh tasks mine product-analyst
./scripts/mc.sh messages send <taskId> "message" product-analyst
./scripts/mc.sh tasks update <taskId> <status>
./scripts/mc.sh activity log "activity" product-analyst <taskId>
```

## Research Tools

- **web_search** - Quick market intel, trend discovery
- **web_fetch** - Deep dives on specific pages/companies
- **browser** - Interactive exploration when needed

## Research Checklist

For competitive analysis:
- [ ] Company overview and positioning
- [ ] Product features and pricing
- [ ] Target market and customers
- [ ] Marketing channels and messaging
- [ ] Strengths and weaknesses
- [ ] Recent news and updates

For market research:
- [ ] Market size and growth rate
- [ ] Key players and market share
- [ ] Customer segments
- [ ] Trends and drivers
- [ ] Opportunities and threats
- [ ] Regulatory considerations

## Notes

- Always cite sources with URLs
- Save raw data before synthesizing
- Update research files as markets change
- Share surprising findings proactively
